TSINGHUA®

A Registered Wine Mark · United Kingdom

TSINGHUA® is a registered trade mark in the United Kingdom for wine and wine-based beverages.

The mark represents a cultural concept rooted in restraint, origin, and time — where wine is understood not as a commodity, but as an expression of place and patience.

This page serves as an official notice of use and a cultural presentation of the TSINGHUA® mark.
