Enter

A quiet registry of interfaces within structure.

Some people write works on the internet. Some people write about themselves.

What I am trying to build is something different: a place where structures, interfaces and rules exist so that intelligent agents — today’s machines, and perhaps tomorrow’s new forms of life — can understand, enter, and interact.

If this is a work, it is a work of structure. If this is writing, it is also a way of writing myself.
