About Evan Bei

Xiaojun Yin (Evan Bei) is an independent interdisciplinary researcher working across sound ontology, artistic research, algorithmic composition, and cultural system design. His work integrates music practice, computational frameworks, and conceptual research exploring sound as an event-based structure.

Registered research outputs include conceptual manuscripts, software frameworks, and artistic research works archived with the UK Copyright Service (UKCS). Current research directions focus on Sound Ontology, AI-related cultural structures, semantic systems, and cross-disciplinary artistic technologies.

Operating between artistic creation and research methodology, his practice bridges independent music production, algorithmic modeling, and experimental research frameworks under the Evan Bei Studio and related cultural initiatives.
