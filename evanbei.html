Research Focus

Evan Bei works at the intersection of sound studies, time-based practice, and conceptual research. Sound is approached not as a fixed object but as an event articulated through temporal decision, embodied conditions, and contextual relations.

Core Project

Sound as Event develops a conceptual framework for understanding sound in event-based terms rather than as an object.

  • Breath as Event — embodied conditions of initiation
  • Pulse as Event — temporal decision structures
  • Voice as Event — execution and presence
